PUP Gymnasium

The Gymnasium at PUP Sta. Mesa is a central facility for sports and recreational activities on campus. It is primarily used for a wide range of events, including intercollegiate sports competitions, physical education classes, and various school-wide athletic activities. The gymnasium is equipped to host different sports like basketball, volleyball, and badminton, and is frequently the venue for tournaments, practice sessions, and training for student-athletes. Additionally, it is used for large-scale events such as university-wide assemblies, concerts, and other activities that bring the PUP community together.

Apart from supporting athletics, the PUP gymnasium also offers students and staff a place to engage in physical fitness activities and extracurricular pursuits. It plays an important role in fostering school spirit, teamwork, and a healthy lifestyle. Access to the gymnasium may be available during specific hours, with priority given to scheduled sports events or classes. For casual use, students and staff may need to check the schedule or reserve the space in advance, especially during high-demand periods.
