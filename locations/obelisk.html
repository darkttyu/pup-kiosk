PUP Obelisk

The Obelisk at PUP Sta. Mesa is one of the notable landmarks on the campus. It stands as a symbol of the university’s rich history, values, and commitment to academic excellence. The obelisk is often associated with the university’s identity and pride, serving as a visual representation of the institution's enduring legacy in providing quality education to its students. It is a prominent feature near the main gate or central areas of the campus, making it easily recognizable for both students and visitors.

The PUP Obelisk is not just a decorative structure but also a symbol of unity and pride for the PUP community. It often serves as a gathering point during campus events, ceremonies, and important university celebrations. The obelisk also contributes to the aesthetic of the campus, offering a spot for students and visitors to take photos or reflect. It holds a special place in the hearts of the university community, representing the institution’s long-standing tradition and its continuous journey toward excellence.
