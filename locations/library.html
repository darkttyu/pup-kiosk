PUP Library

The Ninoy Aquino Library and Learning Resources Center (NALLRC) at PUP Sta. Mesa is one of the central hubs of academic support and intellectual development on campus. It houses an extensive collection of books, journals, and other educational materials, serving as a vital resource for students, faculty, and researchers. The library is equipped with modern facilities and offers a quiet, conducive environment for studying, research, and collaborative learning. It is named after former Senator Benigno "Ninoy" Aquino Jr., symbolizing the importance of knowledge and education in shaping the future of the nation.

In addition to its rich physical collection, the NALLRC provides access to digital resources such as online databases, e-books, and academic journals, supporting a wide range of academic disciplines. The library also features study areas, computer stations, and rooms for group discussions, making it a versatile space for individual and collaborative work.
