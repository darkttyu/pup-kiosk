Charlie del Rosario Building

The Charlie Del Rosario Building at PUP Sta. Mesa is an important academic structure on campus, named in honor of a notable figure who contributed to the university’s growth. The building is primarily used for academic purposes and houses several classrooms, lecture halls, and faculty offices. It serves as a central location for many of the university’s programs, offering a conducive environment for learning and collaboration. The building’s design focuses on functionality, providing students and faculty with the necessary space and facilities for teaching, studying, and conducting research.

In addition to its academic function, the Charlie Del Rosario Building often hosts university events, seminars, and workshops, contributing to the dynamic educational atmosphere of PUP Sta. Mesa. It is strategically located to serve various departments, making it easily accessible to students from different academic disciplines. The building is an integral part of campus life, symbolizing the university’s commitment to providing quality education and fostering a vibrant academic community. Its central role in supporting educational activities makes it a significant landmark within the PUP Sta. Mesa campus.
