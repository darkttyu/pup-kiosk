Swimming Pool

The Swimming Pool at PUP Sta. Mesa is another important recreational facility available to students, staff, and sometimes visitors. It serves as a venue for various aquatic activities, including swimming lessons, training for competitive swimmers, and casual recreation. The pool is often used by physical education classes, student-athletes, and those looking to stay fit through swimming. It provides a safe and accessible space for water-based activities and plays an important role in promoting physical fitness and overall well-being within the campus community.

In addition to supporting athletic and academic programs, the swimming pool is also used for university events and competitions. It may be reserved for specific events, such as intercollegiate swimming meets or university-wide recreational programs. Students and staff interested in using the pool may need to follow certain guidelines, such as designated swim hours or reservation procedures, especially during peak times. The pool offers an opportunity for students to engage in low-impact exercise, making it a valuable resource for maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
