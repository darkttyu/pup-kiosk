Basketball and Volleyball Court

The Basketball and Volleyball Courts at PUP Sta. Mesa are key recreational facilities that support the university’s athletic programs and promote physical fitness among students and staff. These courts are often used for intercollegiate sports events, physical education classes, and casual games. They provide an opportunity for students to engage in team sports, which are a significant part of campus life and student activities at PUP.

The basketball and volleyball courts are typically open to students, staff, and sometimes visitors, although access may be subject to scheduling or reservation requirements, particularly for events or official competitions. These courts are integral to fostering a vibrant and active student body, as well as encouraging teamwork and physical well-being. As with other facilities, individuals wishing to use the courts may need to coordinate through the university’s sports office to ensure availability and comply with any campus regulations.
