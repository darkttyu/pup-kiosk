Visitor Information Center

This will be the first building that a student or visitor will see when they enter the main gate of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. The Visitor Information Center is a one-stop shop for all the information that a student or visitor needs to know about the university. It is a place where students can get information about the different colleges, courses, and facilities available in the university. The Visitor Information Center also provides information about the different services that the university offers, such as scholarships, financial aid, and student support services.
