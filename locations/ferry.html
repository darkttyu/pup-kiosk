PUP Ferry Station

The PUP Sta. Mesa Ferry Station, also known as the Sta. Ana Ferry Terminal, is part of the Pasig River Ferry Service, offering an alternative mode of transportation for commuters in Metro Manila. This station provides a convenient route for those traveling between the Sta. Mesa area and other parts of the city, particularly for destinations along the Pasig River. The ferry service is operated by the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA) and is recognized as the only water-based transportation system in Metro Manila.

The PUP Sta. Mesa Ferry Station is accessible via various modes of public transportation. The nearest train station is Paco PNR, approximately an 11-minute walk away. Additionally, bus lines such as BUS and JEEP, as well as the LRT 2 line, have stops near the ferry station.
